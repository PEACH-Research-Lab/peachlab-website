PEACH

Privacy- Enabling AI and Computer- Human interaction Lab

We are a group of researchers passionate about exploring the intersection of HCI, AI, and privacy. We believe that addressing privacy issues raised by AI requires not only model-centered approaches—approaches that improve the models—but also human-centered approaches—approaches that empower people.
